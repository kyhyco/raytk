# lookAtCamera

A camera that focuses on a specific point in space.

## Parameters

* Camera (`Camera`)
* FOV Angle (`Camfov`)
* Position (`Campos`)
* Rotate (`Camrot`)
* Look At Position (`Lookatpos`)
* Up Vector (`Camup`)
* Inspect (`Inspect`)
